Our Objectives

Environmental Care

Planting trees and promoting eco-friendly practices.

Health & Safety

Conducting health awareness camps, medical check-ups in schools, and safety programs.

Education Support

Providing sports and science materials to schools lacking facilities, and encouraging poor and meritorious students.

Inclusive Care

Helping physically handicapped children, supporting old age people, blind and orphan children.

Women Empowerment

Offering tailoring training to women for self-reliance.
